About State Finder

 

The State Finder web application was created in Fall of 2022 as a Senior Capstone project by Alyiah Proctor, Anne Nguyen, Darlyn Mendez, and Emily Crabtree. The goal of the web application is to be a tool to aid people undergoing a career transition, job search, and/or location change, and to make those processes less stressful. The State Finder web application collects user data based on their occupation, job level, and number of working adults and children in the household and compares it with the minimum livable wages of the major Metropolitan areas in the United States of America, as provided by MIT. A red-green choropleth map is produced to visually illustrate which Metropolitan areas the user meets the minimum livable wage.
